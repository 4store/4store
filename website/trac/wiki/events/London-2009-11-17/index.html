events/London-2009-11-17

User Forum

We're organising a social event for 4store users, or potential users, or other interested parties. Garlik are sponsoring the even with some free beer. Feel free to send this invite on to other people who are interested in these technologies.

Upcoming event page:  http://upcoming.yahoo.com/event/4615695

Time
1800-closing
Date
2009-11-17
Location
Skinkers, London, 42 Tooley Street,  SE1 2SZ, near London Bridge tube.  http://www.fancyapint.com/pubs/pub3596.html

Number of people attending so far: 18

Please add yourself to the  upcoming page if you'd like to attend, so we have some idea of the numbers.

We'll provide some food and put some money behind the bar for drinks.

We're also for people who're working with 4store now to give short (10 minute or so) informal talks on what they've done, we've got a few volunteers, but anyone else who's keen to talk would be more than welcome. Please mail me with some details, so we can organise. There's a plasma TV with laptop input in the room we've booked, but no projector.

People who've offered to talk:

  • Tom Ilube (Garlik)
  • Steve Harris (Garlik)
  • Yves R. (BBC)
  • Kurt J. (QMU)
